How to Convert Femtomol/second to Mol/second

1 fmol/s = 1.0E-15 mol/s
1 mol/s = 1.0E+15 fmol/s

Example: convert 15 fmol/s to mol/s:
15 fmol/s = 15 × 1.0E-15 mol/s = 1.5E-14 mol/s
