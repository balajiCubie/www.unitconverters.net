How to Convert Mol/hour to Mol/second

1 mol/h = 0.0002777778 mol/s
1 mol/s = 3600 mol/h

Example: convert 15 mol/h to mol/s:
15 mol/h = 15 × 0.0002777778 mol/s = 0.0041666667 mol/s
