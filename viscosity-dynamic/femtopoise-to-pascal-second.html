How to Convert Femtopoise to Pascal Second

1 fP = 1.0E-16 Pa*s
1 Pa*s = 1.0E+16 fP

Example: convert 15 fP to Pa*s:
15 fP = 15 × 1.0E-16 Pa*s = 1.5E-15 Pa*s
