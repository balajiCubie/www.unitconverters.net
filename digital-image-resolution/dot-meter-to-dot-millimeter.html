How to Convert Dot/meter to Dot/millimeter

1 dot/m = 0.001 dot/mm
1 dot/mm = 1000 dot/m

Example: convert 15 dot/m to dot/mm:
15 dot/m = 15 × 0.001 dot/mm = 0.015 dot/mm
