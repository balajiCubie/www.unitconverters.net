How to Convert Dyne Millimeter to Newton Meter

1 dyn*mm = 1.0E-8 N*m
1 N*m = 100000000 dyn*mm

Example: convert 15 dyn*mm to N*m:
15 dyn*mm = 15 × 1.0E-8 N*m = 1.5E-7 N*m
