How to Convert Cubic Meter/hour to Cubic Meter/second

1 m^3/h = 0.0002777778 m^3/s
1 m^3/s = 3600 m^3/h

Example: convert 15 m^3/h to m^3/s:
15 m^3/h = 15 × 0.0002777778 m^3/s = 0.0041666667 m^3/s
