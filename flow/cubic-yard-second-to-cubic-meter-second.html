How to Convert Cubic Yard/second to Cubic Meter/second

1 m^3/s = 1.3079506193 yd^3/s

Example: convert 15 yd^3/s to m^3/s:
15 yd^3/s = 15 × 0.764554858 m^3/s = 11.4683228703 m^3/s
