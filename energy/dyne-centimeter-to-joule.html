How to Convert Dyne Centimeter to Joule

1 dyn*cm = 1.0E-7 J
1 J = 10000000 dyn*cm

Example: convert 15 dyn*cm to J:
15 dyn*cm = 15 × 1.0E-7 J = 1.5E-6 J
