How to Convert Kilowatt-second to Gigajoule

1 kW*s = 1.0E-6 GJ
1 GJ = 1000000 kW*s

Example: convert 15 kW*s to GJ:
15 kW*s = 15 × 1.0E-6 GJ = 1.5E-5 GJ
