How to Convert Attojoule/second to Watt

1 aJ/s = 1.0E-18 W
1 W = 1.0E+18 aJ/s

Example: convert 15 aJ/s to W:
15 aJ/s = 15 × 1.0E-18 W = 1.5E-17 W
