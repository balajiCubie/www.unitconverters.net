How to Convert Femtohertz to Hertz

1 fHz = 1.0E-15 Hz
1 Hz = 1.0E+15 fHz

Example: convert 15 fHz to Hz:
15 fHz = 15 × 1.0E-15 Hz = 1.5E-14 Hz
