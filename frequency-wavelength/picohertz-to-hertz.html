How to Convert Picohertz to Hertz

1 pHz = 1.0E-12 Hz
1 Hz = 1000000000000 pHz

Example: convert 15 pHz to Hz:
15 pHz = 15 × 1.0E-12 Hz = 1.5E-11 Hz
