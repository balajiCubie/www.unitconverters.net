How to Convert Attohertz to Hertz

1 aHz = 1.0E-18 Hz
1 Hz = 1.0E+18 aHz

Example: convert 15 aHz to Hz:
15 aHz = 15 × 1.0E-18 Hz = 1.5E-17 Hz
