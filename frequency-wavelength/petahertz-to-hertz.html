How to Convert Petahertz to Hertz

1 PHz = 1.0E+15 Hz
1 Hz = 1.0E-15 PHz

Example: convert 15 PHz to Hz:
15 PHz = 15 × 1.0E+15 Hz = 1.5E+16 Hz
