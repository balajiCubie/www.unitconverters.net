How to Convert Exahertz to Hertz

1 EHz = 1.0E+18 Hz
1 Hz = 1.0E-18 EHz

Example: convert 15 EHz to Hz:
15 EHz = 15 × 1.0E+18 Hz = 1.5E+19 Hz
