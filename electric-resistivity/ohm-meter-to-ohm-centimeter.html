How to Convert Ohm Meter to Ohm Centimeter

1 ohm meter = 100 ohm centimeter
1 ohm centimeter = 0.01 ohm meter

Example: convert 15 ohm meter to ohm centimeter:
15 ohm meter = 15 × 100 ohm centimeter = 1500 ohm centimeter
